View Details

Event Details

We are pleased to invite you to our Annual Community Iftar Gathering during the blessed month of Ramadan. This event brings together families, friends, and community members to share in the breaking of the fast and enjoy a delicious meal together.

The evening will include Maghrib prayer, a nutritious iftar meal, and an inspirational talk by our guest speaker, Imam Abdullah Hassan. This is a wonderful opportunity to strengthen community bonds and experience the spirit of Ramadan together.

All are welcome to attend. The event is free, but registration is required to help us prepare adequately.
